New York, NY Active

Overview

We are seeking an experienced Senior UI/UX Designer to craft beautiful and intuitive web experiences. You will be responsible for the end-to-end design process, creating high-fidelity mockups, and collaborating closely with our engineering team to ensure pixel-perfect implementation.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design high-fidelity mockups for web and mobile applications.

  • Conduct user research and usability testing.
  • Maintain and expand our comprehensive design system.
